Project Overview
A Visitor Management System (VMS) is a secure web-based application that digitizes
the process of managing visitors entering an organization. Instead of maintaining paper
registers, visitors can register online or at a reception kiosk, employees receive approval
requests instantly, security personnel verify visitor identity using QR codes, and all visitor
activities are logged for security and auditing purposes.
The system improves security, efficiency, visitor experience, and compliance by
maintaining real-time visitor records and automating the complete check-in/check-out
workflow.
Objectives
Eliminate manual visitor registers.
Improve office security.
Enable quick visitor approvals.
Reduce waiting time at reception.
Generate digital visitor passes.
Track visitor movements inside the organization.
Maintain complete security audit logs.

Expected Outcome
The Visitor Management System provides a secure, paperless, and efficient way to
manage office visitors. By integrating employee approvals, QR-based check-in/check-out,
meeting room scheduling, and detailed security logs, it streamlines the entire visitor
lifecycle, improves workplace security, reduces manual effort, and delivers real-time
visibility into visitor activities for organizations of any size.
